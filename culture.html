Culture

Holidays and Traditions

Lunar New Year

Lunar New Year also known as Chinese New Year is an important holiday that we celebrate in my culture. Its date is coincided with the date of the start of the new lunarsolar calendar. During this holiday, we celebrate by meeting with relatives to have a meal together. We also visit each other's homes to greet them and to wish them good luck in the new year. Other common traditions that take place during this holiday include dragon boats, dragon dancing, and handing out red envelopes to young relatives.

Mid Autumn Festival

The Mid Autumn Festival is held on the 15th day of the 8th month of the lunar calendar and therefore usually takes place in September or October.. One key traditon during this time is eating moon cake whic hare small round cakes usually filled with lotus seed paste and eggs enveloped in an hard pastry outer layer. In this holiday, we also celebrate have having a family reunion dinner (or meal) to bring happiness and peace.
