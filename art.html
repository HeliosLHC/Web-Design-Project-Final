Chinese Art

Art is an key part of my heritage as my home has many copies of famous chinese artworks.

Several notable chinese painters include

  1. Gu Kaizhi
  2. Zhan Ziqian
  3. Han Gan
  4. Han Huang
  5. Li Sixun

This is an famous painting by renown chinese art Xia Gui.

Paintings of bamboos are extremely common as bamboo are a cherished plant in chinese culture and can be found in many pieces of artwork. I have two bamboo paintings printed on bamboo at home and as a result are familiar with them.
